HOP

Healthcare Operations Platform / The Enterprise Healthcare Operations ERP
Status: Active Platform

HOP is a next-generation Healthcare Operations ERP designed to unify the operational side of modern medical practices into a single intelligent platform. Rather than replacing an Electronic Health Record, HOP extends its capabilities by coordinating the people, departments, workflows, communications, and business operations that surround patient care while preserving the EHR as the authoritative clinical system of record.
